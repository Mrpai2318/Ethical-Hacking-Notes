### Computer Networking

## Classification of Computer Networks
On the basis of their geographic coverage they are of 3 types:-
1. LAN(Local Area Network):- Mostly used in office or a home covers a small area. It provides high speed connection it is usually costing less and is not hard to manage and the devices can be connected wirelessly.
2. MAN(Metropolitan Area Network):- It connects several buildings in a city or between two cities. It has lesser speed than LAN it costs more to setup than LAN and it quite difficult to manage. Eg: CCTV cameras connected accross city.
3. WAN(Wide Area Network):- It connects accross states countries or even continents it is usually made up of LAN and MAN connected to each other. The setup cost is very expensive. Eg: Internet is WAN which anyone and everyone can connect to.

On the basis of accessibility they are of 2 types:-
1. Internal Network
2. External Network
